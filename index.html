Odd or Even

In this game, a player holds a number of marbles in their hand. The other player must guess if the number is odd or even. If the player guessing is correct, he gets the same number of marbles as he’s holding (his wager). If he doesn’t guess correctly, he forfeits the same number of marbles as the other player is holding.
